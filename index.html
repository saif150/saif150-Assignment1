Admission process

📅 Admission Open: April – June
🏫 Classes: Nursery to XII

How to apply:

  • Collect the admission form from the school office or download it from our website.
  • Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  • Appear for the interaction/interview session.

Documents Required:

  • ✅ Birth Certificate
  • ✅ Previous Report Card
  • ✅ Passport-sized Photographs
  • ✅ Aadhaar Card
